# Skill: write_glossary_entry

**Version**: 1.0.0
**Primary Agent**: EducationDesigner
**Purpose**: Create standardized glossary term definitions (1-3 sentences) with cross-references.

## Inputs
- term (string)
- chapter_introduced (int)
- technical_domain (string)
- related_terms (array)
- mathematical_notation (string, LaTeX)

## Outputs
- Concise definition (1-3 sentences, 50-150 words)
- Synonyms and related terms
- Mathematical notation (if applicable)
- Cross-references

## Success Criteria
- Technically accurate
- 1-3 sentences
- No circular definitions
- Links to related terms
- Standard robotics notation

## Estimated Time: 10-15 minutes per term
